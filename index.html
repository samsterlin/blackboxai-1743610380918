About Our Temple

Kuriyanvila Sree Bhadrakali Temple stands as a testament to centuries of spiritual devotion and architectural brilliance. This sacred temple, dedicated to Goddess Bhadrakali, has been a center of divine worship and cultural heritage.

The temple is renowned for its unique architectural style and the powerful presence of the goddess. The annual festival, celebrated with great devotion, attracts thousands of devotees from across the region.
